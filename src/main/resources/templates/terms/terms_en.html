Objectives of the research project

Several researchers from the Institut de Recherche en Informatique de Toulouse (IRIT) have been working since 2011 to set up the "elaastic" platform that allows teachers to orchestrate sequences of formative assessments during face-to-face or distance learning. In the context of the B4MATIVE research project funded by the Direction du Numérique pour l'Éducation (french government), we make available to teachers and students of the academy Nancy-Metz (project partner), and more generally to any teacher of universities, our web platform "elaastic" to collect and analyze traces from user interactions with elaastic. This collection and the associated analysis are aimed at:

  • a better understanding and a better characterization of what is a "good" formative evaluation sequence orchestrated by a digital platform;
  • the proposal of guidelines for the design of digital platforms dedicated to the orchestration of sequence of formative evaluations.

What is expected from you (methodology)

You are a teacher

If you agree to participate in this study, you will be asked to create questions and assignments on the elaastic platform for questioning your students during class or in the context of homework assignments. You will then be able to use elaastic to manage the different phases of your formative evaluations (phase of answers to questions, phase of confrontation of point of view, phase of restitution).

You are a student

If you agree to participate in this study, you will be asked to answer your teachers 'questions using the elaastic platform and to comment on your classmates' responses. Your contributions to a homework assignment will be used by your teacher to provide guidance to help you improve your understanding of the topics covered in the questions.
